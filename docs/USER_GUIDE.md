# AI Case Interview Simulator - User Guide

## Overview

The AI Case Interview Simulator is a web application designed to help MBA and Master's students practice consulting and product management case interviews. The system uses AI agents to generate cases, conduct interviews, evaluate performance, and provide personalized coaching feedback.

## Getting Started

### Account Creation

1. Navigate to the signup page
2. Create an account with your email and password
3. Log in to access the dashboard

### Dashboard

The dashboard provides an overview of:
- Your interview history
- Recent cases
- Performance statistics (coming soon)

## Using the System

### Step 1: Generate or Select a Case

#### Generate a New Case

1. Click "Generate Case" from the dashboard
2. Enter a topic (e.g., "Airline Profitability", "New Market Entry")
3. Select case type:
   - **Consulting**: Traditional consulting case interview
   - **Product Management**: Product management case interview
4. Click "Generate"
5. The system will create a custom case using AI and RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

#### Use an Existing Case

1. Browse available cases from the cases list
2. Select a case to view details
3. Click "Start Interview" to begin

### Step 2: Start an Interview

1. Choose your interview mode:
   - **Interviewer-Led**: The AI interviewer guides you through the case with structured questions
   - **Candidate-Led**: You drive the structure and ask for data as needed
   - **PM Product Case**: Product management focused interview

2. Click "Start Interview"
3. You'll be taken to the interview interface

### Step 3: Conduct the Interview

#### Chat Interface

- The interview is conducted through a real-time chat interface
- Type your responses in the message box
- Press Enter or click "Send" to submit
- The AI interviewer will respond based on your answers

#### Recording Your Interview (Optional)

1. Click "Start Recording" to begin video/audio recording
2. The recording indicator will show when you're recording
3. Click "Stop Recording" when finished
4. The recording will be automatically uploaded and transcribed
5. Transcription is used for performance evaluation

#### Requesting Exhibits

- Ask for data or exhibits when needed (e.g., "Can I see the financial data?")
- The AI will provide relevant exhibits based on your requests
- Exhibits are released progressively to simulate a real interview

### Step 4: Complete and Evaluate

1. When finished, click "Complete Interview" or "Evaluate"
2. The system will:
   - Analyze your responses
   - Evaluate your performance across multiple dimensions
   - Generate personalized feedback
   - Provide coaching recommendations

### Step 5: Review Feedback

The feedback page shows:

- **Performance Scores** (0-100):
  - Structure: Framework usage and organization
  - Hypothesis: Hypothesis formation and testing
  - Math: Quantitative analysis accuracy
  - Insights: Quality of recommendations

- **Strengths**: What you did well

- **Areas for Improvement**: Specific areas to focus on

- **Recommended Drills**: Practice exercises tailored to your performance

- **Next Steps**: Action items for improvement

## Interview Modes Explained

### Interviewer-Led Mode

- Best for: Beginners or structured practice
- The AI interviewer guides you through the case
- Provides hints if you're stuck
- Asks probing questions to test your thinking
- Similar to traditional consulting interviews

### Candidate-Led Mode

- Best for: Advanced practice
- You drive the structure and analysis
- Ask for data when needed
- The interviewer provides information on request
- Tests your ability to structure problems independently

### PM Product Case Mode

- Best for: Product management interviews
- Focuses on product thinking and user empathy
- Evaluates trade-off analysis and prioritization
- Challenges assumptions about product decisions

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific**: Provide detailed responses rather than brief answers
2. **Show Your Work**: Walk through your calculations and reasoning
3. **Use Frameworks**: Structure your approach (e.g., MECE, 4Ps, Porter's Five Forces)
4. **Ask Questions**: Clarify the problem before jumping to solutions
5. **Request Data**: Don't hesitate to ask for exhibits or additional information
6. **Record Sessions**: Enable recording for communication style analysis (coming soon)

## Troubleshooting

### Recording Issues

- **Camera/Microphone not working**: Check browser permissions
- **Recording not uploading**: Check your internet connection
- **Transcription failed**: The system will retry automatically

### Interview Issues

- **AI not responding**: Refresh the page and try again
- **Connection lost**: Your progress is saved automatically
- **Case generation failed**: Try a different topic or case type

### Performance Issues

- **Slow responses**: The AI may take a few seconds to generate responses
- **Evaluation taking time**: Complex evaluations may take 30-60 seconds
